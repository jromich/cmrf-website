Board Members

Thomas J. Bonasera

Mr. Bonasera is an Attorney with Dinsmore & Shohl, LLP. He has been recognized as one of Ohio's Super Lawyers in 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007 and 2008 as voted by his peers. He focuses on trust and estate administration and litigation, estate planning and closely-held business matters.

Denison Neale Jr.

Mr. Neale has had a successful career for over thirty-five years in the commercial real estate mortgage banking business. He is currently with Bellwether Real Estate Capital.

F. Donald Rothermich

Mr. Rothermich is First Vice President–Investments with Raymond James & Associates, Inc. He has been a successful financial adviser for over thirty-five years. Mr. Rothermich is a past President and Chairman of the Central Ohio Chapter of the Arthritis Foundation and has been an active member of the Board for over forty years. He is the son of CMRF founder, Norman O. Rothermich.

Bruce A. Wall, M.D.

Dr. Wall is a Rheumatologist who previously practiced at the Columbus Medical Center. After additional education, he served in leadership roles at Riverside Methodist Hospital, United HealthCare, and Central Ohio Primary Care. He is currently Chief Medical Officer of The Medical Group of Ohio. Dr. Wall serves on several Boards, including that of the Columbus Medical Association.

Elizabeth A. Tschosik

Dr. Tschosik is an independent health economic and outcomes research consultant for the pharmaceutical/biotech industry, currently associated with Health Outcomes Solutions. Dr. Tschosik has a PhD in Pharmaceutical Administration from The Ohio State University. Dr. Tschosik is the daughter of current CMRF Chairman and Board Member, F. Donald Rothermich.

Richard H. Turner, M.D.

Dr. Turner is a widely-respected Orthopedic Surgeon with over thirty years of experience. He is a past President and long-time member of the Board of the Central Ohio Chapter of the Arthritis Foundation. Dr. Turner was affiliated with Riverside Methodist Hospital and is now retired. Dr. Turner is the son of the former CMRF Board Member, Floyd Turner.
